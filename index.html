Product Information

Our WIY Winch Mount Kit is purpose-built for 1st Gen Toyota Tundras and Sequoias. Engineered for strength and DIY adaptability, this kit includes everything needed to weld and install a custom winch plate behind your stock or aftermarket bumper. Made from heavy-duty steel, the components are CNC cut and shipped raw allowing for full customization and integration with your existing bumper setup.

It’s designed to sit snugly between the frame rails and bolt directly to them using the provided mounting plates, ensuring a secure and solid recovery anchor point. The low-profile design maintains airflow to your radiator while positioning the winch for optimal pulling power and front-end reinforcement.

Whether you're looking to upgrade your recovery game or just want a cleaner, more integrated setup, this kit makes it easy to run a winch without replacing your entire bumper. It also serves as an excellent base for fabricators wanting to build their own front-end protection, bull bars, or custom bumper designs around a strong, pre-aligned winch platform.

Plus, because it’s a weld-it-yourself kit, it gives you total control over how it fits, looks, and functions. From mild trail rigs to hardcore overland builds, this winch mount adapts to your goals and your creativity.
